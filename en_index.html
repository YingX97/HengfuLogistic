Company Profile


Guangzhou Hengfu Logistics Co., Ltd. was established in 2004 with a registered capital of 10 million RMB. The company has more than 20 branches and offices, and a team of over 300 employees who are professional, knowledgeable, and energetic. Our company is an A-class shipping agent approved by the Ministry of Transportation (ISA NO.MOC-VA01368) and one of the first NVOCCs (NVOCC NO.MOC-NV.01392) in China. We are a highly competitive integrated logistics service provider in South China. Our main business includes international freight forwarding and international shipping agency services.

Huasheng (Hong Kong) Shipping Co., Ltd. has currently opened shipping routes between Hong Kong and Nansha, Sanshui and Gaoming in Foshan, Jiujiang, Sanshan, Nangang, Taishan Public Welfare, Xinhui, Yunfu, Zhaoqing (Sanrong, Gaoyao, Sihui, Xingang), Wuzhou, Beihai, and Fangchenggang. In order to reduce costs and better serve customers, we have also opened domestic transit services from Yunfu, Zhaoqing, Foshan (Sanshui), and Jiangmen (Taishan Public Welfare) to Shekou Port. Huasheng (Hong Kong) Shipping Co., Ltd. is a member of the South China Express and a partner of the Guangzhou Port Shuttle Bus.

Currently, our company has established branches in Shenzhen, Foshan, Nanhai, Zhaoqing, Yunfu, Jiangmen, Zhanjiang, Nanning, Wuzhou and other cities, and carries out international shipping agency business in Guangzhou and Shenzhen. We are the general agent of Huasheng (Hong Kong) Shipping Co., Ltd. in Mainland China and have established partnerships with many global major shipping companies, making our domestic and international business networks increasingly complete. "Providing customers with reasonable freight rates and ensuring high-quality service" has always been our company's mission. We deeply understand that the fundamental of modern logistics industry is service-oriented. We adhere to the principles of providing high-quality and efficient service, as well as mutual benefit, honesty and sincerity in our business operations, in order to achieve sustained development of the enterprise.

Our Services

International Freight Forwarding

Hengfu Logistics is qualified as an international freight forwarding agent and non-vessel operating common carrier (NVOCC), with over twenty years of experience in the field of international logistics.

We provide comprehensive freight forwarding services that include sea transportation, customs clearance, and logistics solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our experienced team and extensive network of partners ensure timely and cost-effective delivery of goods to destinations worldwide.

International Shipping Agency

Our company has the qualification for international ship agency, providing traditional ship agency services such as liner, tramp, and tanker agency, protective agency, ship supply, maritime consulting, customs declaration and inspection, chartering and booking, etc. According to customers' needs, we organize project logistics and provide comprehensive integrated logistics product services.

Shipping Routes

We offer container feeder services in the Pan Pearl River Delta region, with ports in Hong Kong, Shekou (Chiwan, Da Chan Bay), and Nansha as the hub, connecting to inland river ports along the Xijiang River to serve the economic hinterland of the Pearl River Delta.

Our company currently operates and manages over thirty container and multi-purpose vessels, which can undertake various types of bulk cargo and container transportation. The maximum cargo capacity of the largest vessel in the fleet is 250X20'TEU/4000DWT.
