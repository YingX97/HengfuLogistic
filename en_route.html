Shipping Routes

Container Vessels

We provide container feeder services in the Pearl River Delta region, with ports such as Hong Kong, Shekou (Chiwan, Da Chan Bay), and Nansha as hubs, connecting inland river ports along the West River to serve the economy of the Pearl River Delta hinterland.

Our company currently operates and manages more than 30 multi-purpose container vessels that can handle various types of bulk and container cargo transportation. The maximum cargo capacity of our vessels is 250x20'TEU/4000DWT.
